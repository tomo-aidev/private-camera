FAQ

Q. Is the shutter really silent?

Yes. This app uses a custom capture method that takes photos and videos without any shutter sound.

Q. Is location data automatically removed?

Yes. GPS location is automatically stripped from photos by default. You can also toggle date/time and device info removal individually in Settings.

Q. I forgot my Private BOX passcode

For security reasons, there is no way to recover or reset your passcode. Reinstalling the app will delete all data in the BOX. Please keep your passcode in a safe place.

Q. Where are my photos stored?

All photos and videos are stored in an encrypted area within the app on your device. Nothing is ever sent to external servers.

Q. Is an internet connection required?

No. This app works completely offline. No network connection is needed.

Q. What is the auto-record feature?

When enabled in Settings, video recording starts automatically the moment you open the app. No buttons to press — capture everything from the first second.

Q. What happens if I delete the app?

Deleting the app permanently removes all photos, videos, and settings stored in the BOX. Please save important data to your Camera Roll before uninstalling.
