Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 12, 2026

Silent Camera & GPS Removal - Kesu Camera ("the App") is designed with user privacy as its top priority. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles information.

Core Principles

☁️ No data is ever sent to external servers
📡 The App works completely offline
👤 No account registration or login required
📊 No analytics or tracking
📢 No advertisements

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect any personal information.

All photos, videos, passcodes, and settings are stored exclusively on your device and are never transmitted externally.

2. Camera & Microphone

The App requests access to your camera and microphone for photo and video capture. These are used solely for in-app recording and no audio/visual data is transmitted externally.

3. Location Data

The App may request location access to allow you to choose whether to include GPS data in photo EXIF metadata. Location data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted externally. By default, location data is automatically removed.

4. Face ID / Touch ID

The App uses Face ID / Touch ID for locked album authentication. Biometric data is processed by iOS Secure Enclave and is never accessible to the App.

5. Data Storage

All data is stored in the app's private directory on your device:

- Photos & Videos: App internal storage (encrypted)
- Passcode: Keychain (iOS secure storage)
- Settings: UserDefaults (app-local)

This data is not accessible by other apps.

6. Data Deletion

You can delete photos and videos at any time within the app. Uninstalling the app permanently deletes all stored data (photos, videos, settings). Passcodes stored in Keychain may persist after app deletion.

7. Third-Party Sharing

The App does not provide, sell, or share user data with third parties. No third-party SDKs, analytics tools, or ad networks are used.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is available without age restrictions. Since it collects no personal information, it does not engage in data collection subject to COPPA or similar regulations.

9. Policy Changes

This policy may be updated as necessary. Significant changes will be communicated through app updates.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please contact us at getonnews@gmail.com.
